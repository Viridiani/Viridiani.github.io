About

My name is Avery, and I am pursuing a B.S. in Electrical Engineering with a focus on power engineering and a minor in Computer Science. This website was one of my first projects, built entirely in pure HTML, JavaScript, and CSS (no libraries like React). I periodically update it with some of my professional and personal projects.


A bit about me: I grew up in Colorado and currently live in Golden, where I attend the Colorado School of Mines (Go Orediggers!). Outside of academics, I enjoy caving and hiking. I also like tinkering with electronics: assembling, disassembling, and modifying devices, which has occasionally resulted in a few ruined boards and exploded capacitors.


Most of my projects shown here are personal projects, although a few projects I have done for school will also be posted here and will be accessible via my GitHub.
